Classroom Grade Proxy Setup

One-time setup — creates a personal grade proxy in your Google account so student scores sync directly to your Google Classroom.

1
Sign in with Google
Uses your account to create the proxy — it will only have access to your own classrooms.
🔑
2
Create & deploy the proxy
Automatically creates an Apps Script project in your Google Drive and deploys it as a web app.
⚙️
3
Activate (one click)
Open the proxy URL once so Google can authorise it to access your Classroom.
✅
Your proxy URL
After clicking Activate: Google will ask you to grant the proxy permission to access your Classroom. Click "Allow", then close that tab — you're done! Your proxy URL has been saved to this browser automatically.
⚠️ One extra step required

Your proxy was deployed, but the Google Classroom API needs to be enabled in its Cloud project before it can post grades. Click the button below — it opens the correct page in Google Cloud Console. Just click Enable, then come back here and click Done.

Open Cloud Console →

The proxy script is saved to your Google Drive under My Drive → Apps Scripts. You only need to do this once per Google account — each teacher in the department runs this page with their own account to get their own personal proxy. Your proxy URL is saved automatically to this browser and used whenever you assign activities from the hub.
